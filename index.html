ORCHID

Origin: Found in tropical regions of Asia, Central, and South America.

Feature: Can grow epiphytically on other plants without harming them.

Description: Known for exotic and intricate blooms, symbolizing luxury and beauty.

PEONY

Origin:Native to Asia, Europe, and Western North America.

Feature: Can live and bloom for over 100 years.

Description:Lush, fragrant flowers symbolizing romance and prosperity

ROSE

Origin:Native to North America, Europe, and Asia.

Feature:Renowned for their complex and rich fragrances.

Description: Classic beauty with various colors, symbolizing love and passion.

CALLA LILLY

Origin: Native to southern Africa.

Feature:The flower is a spathe that surrounds a spadix

Description: Elegant, trumpet-shaped blooms representing purity and beauty.

TULIP

Origin:Originated in Central Asia, particularly Turkey and Kazakhstan.

Feature: Can continue growing after being cut.

Description: Versatile and vibrant flowers symbolizing perfect love and elegance.

HYDRANGEA

Origin: Native to Asia and the Americas.

Feature: Color changes based on soil pH.

Description: Large, round flower heads symbolizing gratitude and abundance.
